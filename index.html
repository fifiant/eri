So who am I ?

I am Fawaz aka @fifiant, a PhD at INRIA. I am interested in Multi-Cloud Computing, distributed systems and middleware. Previously, I was Software Engineer Expert at INRIA, working with the SPIRALS research team from the INRIA Lille Nord Europe research center.
  • Software architect [all major web/Cloud/mobile platforms].
  • devOps [enthusiast].
  • Cloud [evangelist].

Some things I do

I am researcher. I am interested on Cloud Computing, Middleware, Distributed computing,Service Oriented Computing (SOC) and Software engineering. My current and past research addresses questions relating to scalable, high availability, and manageable distributed systems. I am improving the performance of lage scale distributed system within the multi-cloud environnment. Previously, I was working on Complex Event Processing to provide more reliable and reproducible experiments in the evaluation of distributed applications at scale. I was further building a distributed platform-as-a-service called soCloud for managing provisioning, portability, elasticity and high availability of distributed applications across multiple clouds.
